Guests & Speakers

Olaf Scholz

Chancellor of Germany (2021-Present)

Bio: Prior to becoming Chancellor, he served as the Mayor of Hamburg, Federal Minister of Finance, and was a prominent member of the Social Democratic Party of Germany (SPD). Known for his contributions to economic policy.

Angela Merkel

Former Chancellor of Germany (2005-2021)

Bio: Throughout her political career, Angela Merkel left a significant mark on German and European politics. Her leadership was characterized by pragmatism and a commitment to stability, and she played a crucial role in shaping the political and economic landscape of Europe.

The Honourable Mélanie Joly, MP

Minister of Foreign Affairs and Member of Canadian Parliament (2015-Present)

Bio: Currently serving as an MP, Joly has been a strong advocate for promoting and preserving Canadian cultural heritage, supporting creative and cultural industries, and advancing bilingualism and multiculturalism in Canada

His Excellency the Honourable Stéphane Dion, PC

Former Canadian Ambassador to Germany (2017-2022)

Bio: Currently Serving as the Canadian Ambassador to France and Monaco. His Excellency was also the Ambassador to Germany from 2017 to 2022. Dion taught Political Science at Université de Moncton in 1984, then at Université de Montréal from 1984 to 1995.
